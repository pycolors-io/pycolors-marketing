## Mental model

> Starter Free helps you validate.
> Starter Pro helps you launch and monetize with less structural work.

## Why this exists

Most developers do not get blocked by the first UI screens.

They get blocked when the product needs:

- real authentication
- billing that does not break
- protected product flows
- backend consistency
- launch confidence

PyColors exists to reduce that phase.

## Decision

If your question is:

> “What should my product look like?”

Start with Starter Free.

If your question is:

> “How do I launch this without losing weeks on auth, billing, and structure?”

Move to Starter Pro.

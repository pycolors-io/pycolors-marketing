## What happens after purchase

When a buyer purchases Starter Pro:

- payment is completed through Stripe
- access is confirmed
- delivery is triggered automatically
- the buyer receives the product without manual intervention

The goal is simple:

> payment → confirmation → access → delivery

That removes friction both for you and for the buyer.

---

## Why this matters

A paid digital product does not feel serious if delivery is fragile.

Buyers want to know:

- payment is secure
- access is controlled
- delivery is immediate
- the product is actually received

Starter Pro includes the foundations required for that experience.

This reduces uncertainty at the most important moment in the buying journey.

---

## Secure by design

Delivery is built with a security-first approach.

Typical foundations include:

- private file storage
- controlled access through the backend
- token-based delivery
- expiring access links
- no public asset exposure

That matters because paid product delivery should not rely on public links or manual fulfillment.

---

## Reliable fulfillment

Starter Pro is designed so delivery does not depend on manual steps.

The flow is shaped for:

- automated fulfillment
- backend-controlled access
- retry-safe processing
- resilience against interrupted browser sessions

That gives you a stronger operational path from checkout to download.

## What Starter Pro is

Starter Pro is a **production-ready SaaS foundation**.

It helps you move from:

- product-shaped validation
- mocked business flows
- frontend-only exploration

to:

- real users
- real billing
- real protected access
- real backend structure
- real delivery after purchase

---

## The problem it solves

Most SaaS projects slow down at the same point:

> “The UI is good enough. Now I need auth, billing, and backend.”

That phase is usually:

- time-consuming
- security-sensitive
- revenue-critical
- easy to partially get wrong

Starter Pro exists to remove that repeated implementation cost.

Without it, teams often lose days or weeks on:

- authentication edge cases
- Stripe billing logic
- session and route protection
- backend structure
- delivery and fulfillment flows

That is exactly the layer Starter Pro is designed to reduce.

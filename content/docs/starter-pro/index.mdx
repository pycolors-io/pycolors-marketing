## Turn a product-shaped app into a real SaaS

Starter Pro is the production layer that helps you move from **validated UI** to **real SaaS infrastructure**.

Starter Free helps you shape the product surface.

Starter Pro helps you launch with:

- real authentication
- real billing
- backend-ready structure
- protected routes
- secure delivery
- production-oriented SaaS flows

It is not just more UI. It is the layer that removes the engineering work that usually delays revenue.

## What Starter Pro solves

Most SaaS projects slow down at the same point:

> The UI is credible. Now auth, billing, backend, deployment, and protected access must become real.

That phase is:

- time-consuming
- security-sensitive
- revenue-critical
- easy to get partially wrong

Starter Pro exists to reduce that repeated implementation cost so you can spend more time on product differentiation.

## Builder path

Most builders follow this path:

1. start with Starter Free
2. validate UX and product structure
3. move to Starter Pro
4. understand the architecture
5. configure environment variables
6. wire authentication
7. wire billing
8. connect backend logic
9. validate delivery and deployment
10. launch

Starter Pro is the bridge between:

> this looks like a SaaS

and:

> this can become a real SaaS business

### What you still build

Starter Pro is a foundation, not your final product.

You still own:

- your product logic
- your domain model
- your workflows
- your onboarding
- your positioning
- your differentiation

That is the point. Starter Pro removes repeated infrastructure work so you can focus on what customers actually pay for.

## Why Starter Pro exists

You are not buying “more code”.

You are buying:

- time saved
- launch risk reduced
- cleaner execution
- fewer auth and billing edge cases
- faster path to revenue
- stronger production confidence
